I work under Design Thinking methodology and I would collaborate with you
from the beginning to the end its steps:

EMPHATIZE

Understanding the stakeholders

DEFINE

State the problem or challenge

IDEATION

Think as many solutions and ideas as possible

PROTOTYPE

Turn those ideas into tangible materials

TESTING

Test how effective are the solutions, then improve them
